Bisleri

Bisleri was originally an Italian alcohol remedy drink created by 19th century inventor Felice Bisleri. In 1965, Italian doctor Cesari Rossi and Indian businessman Khushroo Suntook introduced Bisleri bottled water in India by setting up a factory in Thane

Vedica

Blessed with a fine balance of naturally occuring Calcium, Magnesium, Sulphate and Bi-Carbonates, Vedica gushes from an underground spring and is packed at source in Uttaranchal.
